Using Dimensions

LibreCAD is equipped with several dimensioning tools to help you dimension lines, circles, arcs, and angles. See Current Drawing Preferences for an overview on how to modify the appearance of dimensions.

Configuring Dimension preferences

Before you begin adding dimensions to your drawing, ensure that your dimension preferences are configured.

  1. Click Options.
  2. Click Current Drawing Preferences.
  3. Click Dimensions.
  4. Ensure all properties listed on this page are appropriate for your drawing.
  5. Click OK to save your changes for this drawing only, or click Update Template to use these preferences for future drawings.

Ensuring dimensions are visible in ProNest

You can configure your layers and ProNest Settings to ensure that dimension information is visible on display-only layers in ProNest.

  1. In ProNest, ensure that you have a display-only layer in your Importing Parts Settings (Settings > Importing Parts > CAD Import tab > Display Only).
  2. In LibreCAD, ensure that the Display Only layer exists in your drawing Layers. It may be helpful to change the color of this layer so a process layer isn't confused with the display only layer.
  3. Draw dimensions on the Display Only layer you've added in LibreCAD.
  4. Import the part into ProNest.

Display-only information appears for a part on the nest but isn't visible from the Part List. Additionally, you can view the Part Report for an individual part (right-click on a part from the Part List, and select Part Report), which shows display-only information.

Modifying dimension drawing preferences

Modify properties of all dimension text, lines, arrows, and ticks in the drawing.

  1. Click Options > Current Drawing Preferences > Dimensions.
  2. Modify any parameters.
  3. Click OK to save your changes, or Cancel to reject changes.

Any changes made to Dimensions Drawing Preferences will apply to all existing dimensions in the current drawing and any new drawings created after saving your changes.

  • Use the Angular dimension tool ( ), and select the two line between which you are dimensioning the angle.

Adding individual leader lines

Dimensioning tools automatically insert leaders based on your Current Drawing Preferences. If needed, you can add additional leader lines using the Leader dimension tool ( ).

Moving dimension text

Move the dimension number, but not the dimension lines or arrows.

  1. Click the dimension entity.
  2. Click and hold the blue handle in the middle of the dimension number, then drag and drop to place the text in a new location.

Moving the placement of dimensions

Move the placement of the entire dimension, including the dimension number, lines, and arrows. This may separate the dimension from the shape.

  1. Click the dimension entity.
  2. Click and hold the blue handle at the bottom of the dimension line that is closest to the shape, then drag and drop to place the dimension in a new location.

Extending or shortening dimension lines

Extend or shorten the length of dimension lines. This will keep the dimension lines attached to the shape.

  1. Click the dimension entity.
  2. Click and hold the blue handle beside a dimension arrow, then drag and drop to resize the dimension line.
