Toolbar creator widget

LibreCAD allows you to create custom toolbars that you can dock for quick access to your favorite tools.

Create a new custom toolbar

  1. Open Widgets > Toolbar Creator. The Toolbar Creator window will appear.
  2. In the left pane, click any tool then click the right-pointing chevron (>) to add it to your toolbar list in the right pane. Selecting a category from the left dropdown menu will filter the tools that appear on the left pane.
    • Drag and drop items to re-order them in your new toolbar in the right-hand pane.
    • Remove items from your new toolbar by selecting the tool item then selecting the left-pointing chevron (<).
  3. Once you've added all desired tools, be sure to name your new toolbar in the Name field.
  4. Click Create.

You may need to restart LibreCAD to see your new toolbar appear in the Widgets > Toolbars list and the right-click > Toolbars list.

Modify an existing custom toolbar

  1. Open Widgets > Toolbar Creator. The Toolbar Creator window will appear.
  2. Select from the dropdown menu on the right-hand side the custom toolbar that you want to modify.
  3. Make any changes to the items on your toolbar or their order in the toolbar.
  4. Click Create to save your changes, or exit from the window if you wish to discard your changes.

Delete a custom toolbar

  1. Open Widgets > Toolbar Creator. The Toolbar Creator window will appear.
  2. Select from the dropdown menu on the right-hand side the custom toolbar that you want to delete.
  3. Click Destroy.
