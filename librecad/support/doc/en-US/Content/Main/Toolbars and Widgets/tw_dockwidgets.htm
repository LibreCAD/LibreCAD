About dock widgets

Dock widgets serve two purposes: they provide alternative methods to access drawing tools, and they house additional tools or features:

  • Block list
  • Command line
  • Layer list
  • Library browser
  • Pen wizard

Dock widgets are available for these Drawing Tools:

  • Circle
  • Curve
  • Dimension
  • Ellipse
  • Info
  • Line
  • Modify
  • Polyline

Dock Widget Areas

Widgets in the left, right, top or bottom areas can be  placed above or below existing widgets, creating tabs for each category of widget placed in that area, or place above or below the existing widget dividing the area in sections.

Tool Icon Description
Left Show/hide the dock widgets located on the left side of the drawing window.
Right Show/hide the dock widgets located on the right side of the drawing window.
Top Show/hide the dock widgets located on the top of the drawing window.
Bottom Show/hide the dock widgets located on the bottom of the drawing window.
Floating Show/hide the dock widgets floating within the drawing window or outside of the drawing application.

Moving a dock widget

  1. Click and drag the title bar of a dock widget. As you drag around the widget, notice that a colored preview box appears where the widget can be placed.
  2. Release your mouse to drop the widget, and it will snap in place.

Resizing a dock widget

  • Dock widgets can be resized by selecting and dragging the edge of the widget box to a minimum of five icons width and no shorter than the default height.
